Welcome to iSING Lab!

iSING Lab (intelligent System and networkING Lab) is from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at HKUST, led by Prof. Kai Chen.

The team at iSING Lab focus on the following research areas:

  • Data Center Networking
  • High-performance Networking
  • Networked Systems
  • Machine Learning Systems
  • Privacy-preserving Computing
We have openings for PhD students and PostDocs with research interests in the areas above. You are welcome to contact Prof. Chen for details.
